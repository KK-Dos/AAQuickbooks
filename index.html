About Us


Bradley and Company Inc. began as a small passion project in 2009. We knew that companies were dedicating far too much time and energy to bookkeeping, so we committed to conceptualizing ways that we could facilitate and streamline the process. By taking on the stress that often comes with organizing accounting and financial systems, we’d allow companies we believed in to redirect their focus to growing and sustaining their businesses. Since then, our hard work and dedication has allowed us to grow substantially. Our clients range from mental health professionals to acupuncturists and humanitarian aid providers throughout the world, all of whom can attest to the consistent quality and personable nature of our services. Today, we remain steadfastly committed to partnering with companies whose missions we believe in and that understand our collective responsibility to our environment and global communities. Together, there’s no limit to what we can achieve.
